Visualizing the Link Between Prevalence of Undernourishment (POU) vs. Political Stability in Africa (2023).


PoU stands for Prevalence of Undernourishment(%) and is represented by the country brown gradient fill colors


Political Instability (PI Score) is shown through circle markers and on clicking the circle you get the political Stability Index. The lower the PI the high the poilitical instability
